Why Grow Microgreens?

Discover the incredible benefits of incorporating microgreens into your diet and lifestyle.

Nutrition Powerhouse

Microgreens contain up to 40x more nutrients than their mature counterparts. Packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

Grow Anywhere

Our kits allow you to grow fresh greens year-round, even in small apartments. No garden or special equipment needed.

Fast & Easy

Harvest in just 7-14 days! Our simple system makes growing microgreens effortless for everyone.

Health Benefits of Microgreens

  • Rich in antioxidants that help fight free radicals
  • High concentration of vitamins C, E, and K
  • Support heart health and reduce cholesterol
  • May help reduce risk of chronic diseases
  • Support healthy digestion and gut microbiome

How Our Kits Work

Simple steps to fresh, homegrown microgreens

1

Prepare

Set up your growing tray with the included substrate

2

Sow

Evenly spread the seeds and lightly cover with soil

3

Water

Mist daily to keep the soil moist but not soggy

4

Harvest

Cut your greens in 7-14 days when they're 2-3 inches tall

Our Story

Dimiana was founded in Varna, Bulgaria with a simple mission: to make fresh, nutritious food accessible to everyone, regardless of their living situation or gardening experience.

What started as a small project in our apartment has grown into a movement helping hundreds of Bulgarians take control of their nutrition through homegrown microgreens.
